Instructions for PDFs:

- Place your PDF files in this /pdfs/ folder.
- Update the src attribute in research-template.html to point to your PDF, e.g., src="pdfs/your-research.pdf".
- Use <embed> or <iframe> for embedding.
- To add new research pages: Duplicate research-template.html, update title, background, and PDF src.
- Alternatively, extend the JS in script.js to handle more projects.
